EXPLANATION

Poetic devices is essential tools, the poetry’s heart,  used in enhancing the poem’s meaning and making it a great effect, so the poetry looks charming to be heard and full of secrets.

Here are poetic devices :

1. Rhyme

The two words that have similar sounds implied in the end of lines or within lines

2. Repetition

The repeating of the words, phrases and lines or it is usually called parallelism

3. Personification

A figure of speech in which the inanimate objects, things, animals are connected with the living qualities, the human traits or they have an ability like the human does.

4. Hiperbole

An exaggerative  statement for emphasis.

5. Simile  

A comparison made between two different things but at least there is a point in common. It’s commonly identified by the words “like”, “as”, “or.”

6. Alliteration

The repetition of consonant sounds at the beginning of every words which is placed side by side on the same line 

7. Symbol

A word, an image that reveals  complex values, abstract ideas.  Symbol is defined as something that has the wide meaning and it is influenced by a cultural concept.

8. Assonance  

The repetition of vowel sounds of every words which is placed side by side on the same line.

9. Imagery

It is connected  with all of the human sense, sensory experiences through seeing (visual), hearing ( auditory), touching (tactile), moving (kinaesthetic), smelling (olfactory), and internal sensation ( feeling ).

10. Oxymoron

A  combination of  two words which have  a contradiction meaning. BASED ON YOUR ABOVE QUESTION
