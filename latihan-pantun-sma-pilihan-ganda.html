4. Soal narrative text pilihan ganda sma dan jawabannya


Narrative Text

One day in the forest which was still green and beautiful, the horse at that time still had horns. He proudly reflected in the clear lake in the middle of the forest while admiring his appearance with horns like a king's crown. At that time a deer appeared, which at that time had no horns, joined the mirror and drank in the lake. However, he was immediately chased away by the horse who felt that the deer only interfered with the view and interfered with the enjoyment. The deer sadly left.

Suddenly, from a distance, there was the sound of a tiger roaring loudly, as if to shake the entire dwellers of the quiet forest. The birds were startled flying, all the forest dwellers were hiding in fear. The horse and deer fled as fast as they could. The deer are agile and able to run among the bushes and tree branches. But unfortunately the horse horns caught in a tree branch and screamed for help. Nobody wants to help.

Suddenly, from the bush, unexpectedly a deer appeared, quietly nibbling on a tree branch that was wrapped around a horse's horn. The horse was touched and did not expect the deer's kindness.

When they finally got out of the danger, the horse found it helpful and presented its horns to the deer in gratitude. That's the origin of the deer having antlers.

Who still had horns at that time?

a. The deer

b. The horse

c. The tiger

d. The branch

Why did the horse chase away the deer?

a. He hated the deer.

b. The deer wasn’t pretty.

c. The deer disturbed his enjoyment.

d. The horse was sad and want to be alone.

Why did the horse and the deer run away?

a. they wanted to test who was the faster one.

b. the tiger chased them.

c. the tiger attacked them.

d. They were afraid of the roaring sound of the tiger.

Who finally came to help the horse?

a. The birds.

b. The tiger.

c. The deer.

d. The bush.

Where did the scene happen?

a. It happened in the bush.

b. It happened in a lake.

c. It happened in a green forest.

d. It happened in a zoo.

Pembahasan

Narrative text adalah teks yang menceritakan urutan kejadian yang dialami pelaku dalam teks tersebut, baik itu fiksi maupun non fiksi.

Dalam soal di atas yang dijadikan narrative text adalah cerita fiksi berbentuk fabel yang diperankan oleh binatang penghuni hutan. Dalam narrative text ada empat unsur, yaitu:

Orientation yang merupakan pengenalan awal cerita. Complication yang merupakan awal datangnya masalah. Resolution yaitu pemecahan masalah yang dialami oleh tokoh cerita. Re-orientation yaitu kesimpulan atas seluruh cerita atau berisi pesan cerita tersebut. Pelajari lebih lanjut

           Jellyfish have stomachs and mouths, but no heads. They have nervous systems for sensing the world around them, but no brains. They are made almost entirely of water, which is why you can look through them.

           Some jellyfish can glow in darkness by making their own light. The light is made by a chemical reaction inside the jellyfish. Scientists believe jellyfish glow for several reasons. For example, they may glow to scare away predators or to attract animals they like to eat.

           Most jellyfish live in salt water, apart from a few types that live in fresh water. Jellyfish are found in oceans and seas all over the world. They live in warm, tropical seas and in icy waters near the North and South poles.

25. Andri: “Some of our friends will join the concert tomorrow. Would you like to go with me? What is your opinion about this?”

Sinta: “……… It will be very interesting. I need to refresh my mind this time.”

The suitable expression to complete the dialogue is………

Jawaban: a. What a great idea!

Penjelasan: Pada percakapan lanjutan, Sinta nampak setuju dan tertarik dengan ide pergi menonton konser bersama Andri dan teman-teman lainnya, sehingga ungkapan yang tepat untuk melengkapi adalah ‘what a great idea!’


26. Choose the irregular plural of the following word!

Jawaban: b. goose

Penjelasan: Irregular plural adalah sebuah kata benda yang artinya tidak menjadi jamak jika ditambahi akhiran -s atau -es. Sebagai contoh kata teacher, bentuk jamaknya adalah teachers, berarti kata tersebut bukan irregular plural. Dari semua pilihan jawaban yang tepat adalah goose, karena bentuk goose jamak menjadi geese.


27. Choose the regular plural of the following word!

Jawaban: c. boy

Penjelasan: Regular plural adalah kata benda yang jika ditambahi akhiran -s atau -es menjadi bentuk jamak. Pada pilihan jawaban man, tooth ox dan woman merupakan irregular plural, maka jawaban yang tepat adalah boy => boys.


28. “We use singular verb forms with school subjects.”

Choose the incorrect sentence according to this rule!

Jawaban: a. Maths isn’t my favorite subject

Penjelasan: Kalimat yang tidak tepat berdasarkan pernyataan di atas adalah A karena maths seharusnya ditulis dalam bentuk mathematics. Sementara itu nama mata pelajaran lainnya sudah benar dalam bentuk singular (tunggal)
