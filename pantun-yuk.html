Itu sekali turun dikali 10 dan sekali naik dibagi 10. Tapi kalau ada pangkat kubik (x³), jadinya dikali atau dibagi 1000 (0 nya ada tiga)

Jadi,

(24 × 1000) - (24.000 ÷ 1000)

7.  17l - 350 cl = ... ml

= (17 × 1000) - (350 × 10)

= 17000 ml - 3500 ml

= 13500 ml

8. 60.000 dm³ + 7 dam³ = ... m³

= (60.000 ÷ 1000) + (7 × 1000)

= 60 m³ + 7000 m³

= 7060 m³

9. 38 dal + 54 l = ... dl

= (38 × 100) + (54 × 10)

= 3800 dl + 540 dl

= 4340 dl

= 2000 l + (50 × 10)

= 2000 l + 500 l

13. yuk bisa yuk.........​


Jawaban

1 was, wasn't

2 were, was

3 was, wasn't

4 was, was

5 were, weren't

Pembahasan

Tense yang dipakai adalah simple past tense.

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang terjadi di waktu lampau (masa lalu).

Contohnya: last night, last week, yesterday, dll.

Pola simple past tense nominal/non-verbal (predikat berupa to be):

(+) Subject + was/were + complement.

(-) Subject + was/were not + complement.

(?) Was/were + subject + complement?

I, he, she, it (singular) = gunakan wasThey, we, you (plural) = gunakan were
