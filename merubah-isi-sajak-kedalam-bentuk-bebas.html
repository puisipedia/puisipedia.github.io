15. Rubahlah Kalimat Ini Kedalam Bentuk Positif,Negatif,Introgatif☺​


Jawaban:

a.

(+) My father is a baker

(-) My father is not a baker

(?) is My father a baker?

b.

(+) I am student

(-) I am not a student

(?) am I student?

c.

(+) she is a nurse

(-) she is not a nurse

(?) is she a nurse?

d.

(+) She is a music teacher

(-) She is not a music teacher

(?) is She a music teacher?

e.

(+) The children are dancers

(-) The children are not dancers

(?) are the children dancers?

PEMBAHASANSimple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan yang dilakukan secara berulang, kebiasaan, rutin dan kebenaran umum (fakta).

Rumus Present Tense

(+) S + Be (is/am/are) + Complement

(-) S + Be (is/am/are) + not + Complement

(?) Be (is/am/are) + S + Complement?

Catatan

Subjek + Be

I + amHe/she/it/my father + isYou/we/they/the children + are
